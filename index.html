Speculative design / solo practice

Artist bio

Think Studio is the solo practice of an artist working through speculative design, research, and object-making.

Their work treats future scenarios as plain material: something to draft, test, and argue with. Across installations, publications, and small systems, the practice studies the everyday politics of tools, care, archives, and damaged environments.
