Thinking Weapons

Thinking Weapons is the participatory + critical design practice of Ron Bronson. He makes events, workshops, and convenings about the speculative now. Not the far-off future, but how we can invent futures to improve life right now.

Design decisions are often mysterious. Instead of designing with people, we keep the bricks and mortar of society distant, and too many decisions about civic life, technology, and public systems are opaque. Thinking Weapons is a provocation towards civil participation and the accessibility of critical ideas.

Colophon, with Digressions

Digression Inexplicably, a Green Bay Packers shareholder.

Built hastily with HTML, CSS, JS, GitHub, and Vercel. Colour is set by a small client-side palette engine that audits its own WCAG contrast.

Type currently in use: Zodiak (display), Switzer (running text), and the system monospace stack (labels).
