Thinking Weapons

Room-sized exercises, printed decks, and gallery popups for thinking together in public.

Thinking Weapons is the participatory practice of Ron Bronson — hands-on exhibitions, civic events, and speculative card games that put service and futures design into people’s hands.

The work runs from gallery popups and city design programs to printed card decks and live, room-sized exercises. Each piece is an invitation: a way for a group of people to rehearse a decision, a future, or a system together, in public.

Selected exhibitions and events are listed on the index. For collaborations or bookings, write contact@ronbronson.com.
